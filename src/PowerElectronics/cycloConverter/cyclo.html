Cycloconverter

Cycloconverter convert input AC voltage at one frequency to output AC voltage at a different frequency through one stage conversion.

Cyclorconverter System

Cycloconverter Firing Scheme

The function of the control circuit is to deliver correctly timed, properly shaped, and firing pulses to the gates of the thryistors in the power coverter so as to generate a voltage of the desired wave shape at the output terminals of a cycloconverter.
